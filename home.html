This is SSK's 1-99 fletching guide

I will be showing you how to get from leel 1 all the way to 99

Arrow shafts until level 10

You will need 5 logs and a knife. Use the knife on the logs and make arrow shafts until level 9

Bronze darts from level 10-23

You will need a bunch of feathers and also 60 bronze dart tips. Use the dart tips on the feathers until you are level 23

Iron darts from level 23-37

You will still need feathers and 110 iron dart tips. Use the iron dart tips on the feathers until you have reached level 37

Steel darts from level 37-52

you will need feathers and 260 steel dart tips. Use the feathers on the dart tips and make steel darts all the way to 52

mithril darts from levels 52-67

you will need feathers and 770 mithril dart tips. Use the feathers on the mithril tips and make mithril darts until you finally reach 67

Adamantite darts from levels 67-81

you will need more feathers and 2190 adamant dart tips. Use the feathers on the dart tips and make adamant darts until you get to level 81

Rune darts from levels 81-90

you will need feathers and 3900 rune dart tips. Use the feathers on the dart tips and make rune darts until you get to level 90

Amethyst darts from levels 90-99

you will need feathers and 7040 amethyst dart tips. Use the darttips on the pm the feathers and make amethyst darts until you reach level 99
